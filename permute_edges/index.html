permute edges

in this step you are trying to get all the yellow edges lined up with their side centers. to do this, turn the top layer until two edges line up. if thety are adjacent then have one solved at the back and one at the right and do this algorithm; R U Ri U R U2 Ri. if they are opposite then do that algorithm then they will be adjacent and you can solve it.
