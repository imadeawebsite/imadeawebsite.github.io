permute corners

the next step is to permute the corners. so a permuted corner is one that is in the right place, even if its not oriented correctly. you can either have no corners, one corner, or four corners permuted. if all four are, you are done. if one is, put that in the bottom right corner of the top layer (as seen from a birds eye view) and do this; U R Ui Li U Ri Ui L. if none are solved then do that algorithm until one is solved and then put that in the right place and do it again.
